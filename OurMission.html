Our Mission

At ChessGurus, our mission is to make chess accessible, engaging, and rewarding for everyone. We aim to empower players of all ages and skill levels to think critically, play confidently, and enjoy the lifelong benefits of the game. Through expert guidance, interactive learning, and a supportive community, we’re here to help every learner unlock their full potential, one move at a time.

Our Co-founders

Officers from the multiple-time award-winning chess team at Arizona College Prep took the initiative to create the 501(c)(3) AZ Chess Gurus Organization in 2024. Motivated by their shared passion for the game and a desire to give back to the community, they envisioned a platform that would make chess accessible, engaging, and meaningful for students of all ages and backgrounds. Drawing on their experience in competitive play, leadership, and mentorship, the co-founders launched the organization to promote critical thinking, strategic learning, and a lasting love for chess across Arizona and beyond.
